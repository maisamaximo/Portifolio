About Me

Hi, my name is Maisa

I am from Brazil and have been living in Portugal since 2021. I have a degree in Computer Science and also completed the Udacity’s Front-End Web Developer - Advanced nanodegree.
I'm a fullstack developer focused on Frontend development and I love spending time coding, using my creativity to create projects with a great with UI / UX user interface and I'm also passionate about learn as much as I can about web accessibility.

During my years of career I had the opportunity to be part of the team of developers that implemented the instant payment system (PIX) in one of the largest banking institutions in Brazil. Currently, Pix is already equivalent to more than 80% of the number of card transactions in Brazil.
